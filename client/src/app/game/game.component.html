How to not die in the mine

Four steps. Zero excuses.

1

Drop your name, enter the mine ⛏

Create a room or join with a 4-letter code. Get everyone in. No download needed.

2

Roll the dice 🎲

Get 1–6 steps. Pick a direction on the d-pad. Clock is ticking — move or get skipped.

3

Land on 💎 to claim it

Must land exactly on it. Passing through doesn't count. First to land wins the diamond.

4

Session scores carry over 📊

Play Again resets the board — not the leaderboard. Keep going until someone rage quits.

5

No endless games ⏰

Host can set a 5, 10, or 20 min game clock. When it hits zero — time's up, highest score wins.
